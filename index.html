How To... Make Chai

1. Fill Water

Fill a saucepan with however many cups of water you want to make your chai in. The ratio is usually about 1:1

2. Boil Water

Put the pan on the stove at medium heat

3. Grate Ginger

Grate approximately one 1.5 in off a clove of frozen ginger straight into the saucepan

4. Tea & Sugar

Wait 2-5 min. Once the water comes to a simmer, add in...

  • 1 tsp of tea leaves / serving
  • 1 tsp of tea leaves / serving

5. Add Milk

Let the water come to a boil. When the tea in about to boil over pour in about 4 oz of milk.

6. Strain

Once the chai has come to a simmer, turn the heat off and strain the chai into a mug
